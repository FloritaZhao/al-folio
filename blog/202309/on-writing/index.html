Writing: A Symphony Orchestrated by "Sense and Sensibility"

September 7, 2023

2022  ·  ai-data   thrive   · 

As someone who manages a blog account that requires at least 200,000 words per year, I have recently embarked on the journey of teaching AI how to write. It is a challenging endeavor because for readers, an article is an "experience" that needs to harmoniously blend sense with sensibility, reason with emotion, logic with sensation, and aesthetic with pleasure.

The fundamental requirement for any article is clarity in logic — articulating points clearly and transparently to allow readers to smoothly follow the author's train of thought, comprehend the viewpoints presented, and eventually resonate with the arguments being conveyed. At the outset of an article, considerate authors generally offer fresh information supplemented with easily understandable and acceptable data to captivate the readers' attention. Gradually, deeper layers of information can be introduced, forming a progressively advancing logical structure. At the end of a logical unit, a concise summary should be provided, offering readers a brief recap before smoothly transitioning to the next logical segment. This structure not only ensures the coherence of the article but also aids readers in better understanding and assimilating the information presented.

However, logic alone is insufficient, for we are all sentient beings. To spur someone into action, emotion often prevails over reason. Indeed, a good article's title often instills a specific anticipated emotion in readers — be it "anger," "curiosity," "concern," or "pride." It is the emotion that triggers the "action" of clicking to read further — congratulations, you have successfully completed the first step. Given the presence of this emotion, the beginning of the article should effectively embrace it, responding to the readers emotionally rather than suppressing or ignoring it. A classic example is the opening of "One Hundred Years of Solitude," where the narrative transcends past, present, and future, oscillating between certainty and uncertainty, evoking an emotion that no lens can capture. Throughout the article, the emotional undulations, in tandem with logic, should resemble a riveting movie, with peaks and troughs, tension and relaxation, allowing readers to experience a spectrum of emotional transitions. In the process of "manipulating" emotions, a private understanding is forged with the readers, guiding them towards the action you wish them to undertake.

A well-crafted article should intertwine robust logical threads with touching emotional lines. My next step is to analyze some excellent articles to decipher these two elements, understanding how they organize logical structures and handle emotional narratives. Through such analysis, I believe I can better assist AI in effectively merging logic and emotion to craft outstanding articles.
