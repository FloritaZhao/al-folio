projects

For the past decade, I've been immersed in the endeavor of "doing good by doing doing". Yet, a concerning fact still surrounds us: 99% of the contents/products around us is superficial, often pushing the mantra of "buy, buy, buy" without depth or genuine intent.
It leaves our souls yearning and our minds empty, not considering the broader impact on society and our environment. I intent to create contents with a human touch, sparking genuine emotion and thought.
So, every project I undertake probes deeper questions: Does it enlighten? Challenge status quo? Inspire? Or simply offer newfound knowledge?
Over these years, from sustainability initiatives to innovation districts planning, my mission remains: to enlighten and to inspire.
