Documentary: "Under the Dome"

Billions of Views, achieved through simple but total truth

1: What is this Project About?

This project is a collaboration between Chai Jing's team and the Friends of Nature team, aiming to raise awareness about China's severe smog issue through the medium of animation. The greatest challenge we face is how to convey the gravity of the smog problem in a straightforward and intuitive manner, especially in an age of information overload. Moreover, we aim to not only inform but also inspire people to take concrete actions.

Chai Jing's review: Under the Dome – Investigating China’s Smog

2: What is Our Perspective?

We believe that environmental issues are not distant topics but are closely related to everyone's daily life. Therefore, our animations avoid "preaching" and focus on "genuine intentions and actions."
We start with small actions that everyone can take and communicate to the public in a lively yet meaningful way how they can contribute to environmental protection.

3: What Have We Achieved

Upon its release, "Under the Dome" became a phenomenal work, garnering billions of views.
This is precisely because it touched on a pain point for the Chinese public—smog. Through animation, we successfully presented this severe issue in an intuitive and easily understandable manner. This not only enhanced people's awareness of environmental issues but also stimulated their willingness to take action.
