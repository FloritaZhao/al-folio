WELL™ Building Standard in China

Multifaceted Strategy to Raise Awareness and Encourage its Widespread Adoption

1: Project Overview

This initiative aims to popularize and implement the "WELL™ Building Standard" in China. WELL™ focuses on enhancing the health and well-being of individuals through the built environment.
Our goal is to raise awareness about the importance of the WELL™ standard and encourage its widespread adoption in China.

My Role in the project within FTA: partner relationship development, creative director, designer.

3: Our Approach

We believe that the WELL standard is not merely a set of design guidelines but represents a transformative approach to living and working.
To this end, we've adopted a multifaceted promotional strategy that includes visual campaigns, media collaborations, press conferences, and in-depth partnerships with upstream and downstream suppliers as well as real estate developers.
Our goal is to raise awareness about the importance of the WELL™ standard and encourage its widespread adoption in China.

4: What Have We Achieved

We successfully designed a WELL-compliant office space for WELL's Beijing headquarters and have incorporated WELL design principles into various urban planning and architectural projects.
Additionally, we have actively advocated for our clients to adopt the WELL Building Standard, receiving overwhelmingly positive feedback.
